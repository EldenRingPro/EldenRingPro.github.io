About MCAST

The Malta College of Arts, Science and Technology (MCAST) is a leading vocational education and training institution in Malta. Established in 2001, MCAST offers a wide range of courses and programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in today's competitive job market. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry partnerships, MCAST is committed to providing high-quality education and training that meets the needs of both students and employers.
